Covid Restrictions

One of the requirements from our parents that this event can go ahead on their farm is that it must be legal from a Covid point of view. This means we are allowed a maximum of 30 people.

If you're a member of the Corleychella Plan B WhatsApp group then you're invited! Please let us know if you're able to make it so we know the numbers.
